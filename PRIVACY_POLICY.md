# Privacy Policy for Ethy

**Last Updated: May 10, 2026**

Ethy ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform. We comply with the Law of Ukraine "On Protection of Personal Data" and strive to adhere to the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Personal Data
We collect information that you voluntarily provide when you register or update your profile:
- **Identity Data**: Name, email address, phone number.
- **Social Media**: Optional links to social media profiles.
- **Volunteer Verification**: If you apply for volunteer status, we collect a photo of your ID document and your organization's name for verification purposes.

### B. User Content
We collect content you create on the platform:
- Photos and descriptions included in help requests.
- Photos and text included in fulfillment reports.

### C. Technical and Usage Data
- **Authentication**: We use **HTTP-only cookies** to manage sessions and differentiate user roles (User vs. Administrator).
- **Geolocation**: We provide a feature to obtain your coordinates via your browser/device to help place requests on the map. This is optional; you may also manually select any location on the map.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:
- Identify and verify users and volunteers.
- Facilitate communication through real-time chats.
- Display help requests on an interactive map.
- Send notifications regarding request statuses via email.
- Prevent fraud and maintain community safety.

---

## 3. Data Storage and Security

- **Storage**: Your data is hosted on **Microsoft Azure** cloud servers.
- **Security**: Passwords are encrypted using a secure **hashing algorithm with a unique salt**. We use HTTP-only cookies to prevent XSS attacks.
- **Integrity**: We implement technical measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or alteration.

---

## 4. Data Retention and Deletion

- **Retention**: We retain your personal data as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with services.
- **Soft Delete**: By default, when you delete an account or request, it is marked as "soft deleted" in our system for security and auditing purposes.
- **Full Deletion**: Users may request full erasure of their personal data. To maintain the integrity of the platform's history (e.g., records of completed help requests), we may **anonymize** your data instead of full deletion. This means all personal identifiers (name, email, phone) will be removed or replaced with non-identifiable placeholders, ensuring the data can no longer be linked back to you.

---

## 5. Third-Party Services

We may share limited data with the following services to ensure platform functionality:
- **Cloud Hosting**: Microsoft Azure.
- **Email Service**: SendGrid or SMTP providers (for notifications).
- **Map Services**: Leaflet/OpenStreetMap (for map rendering).

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

---

## 6. Your Rights

Under GDPR and Ukrainian law, you have the right to:
- **Access**: Request a copy of your data.
- **Rectification**: Update or correct your data.
- **Erasure**: Request the deletion of your personal data ("Right to be Forgotten").
- **Withdraw Consent**: Withdraw your consent for data processing at any time.
